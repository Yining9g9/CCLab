浮生若梦 Life is but a Dream

What endures beyond a thousand years? Not gold nor jade, but the reflection on human existence we find in classics.

红楼梦 Dream of the Red Chamber – China's greatest literary masterpiece – reveals this through its iconic paradox: "Truth becomes fiction when the fiction is true, real becomes non-real when the unreal is real"

Steping into Baoyu's dream, let's beginning the journey of life and fate.

This is not nihilism, but a mirror held to life's transience – what will you see reflected?
